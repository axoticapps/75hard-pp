If you would like to delete your 75 Hard Social account and associated data, you can do so in-app or by request.

## Option 1: Delete in the app (recommended)
1. Open **75 Hard Social**
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Delete account**
4. Follow the prompts to confirm

This will delete your account and associated data tied to your login.

## Option 2: Request deletion by email
Email us from the email address used for your account:

**To:** axoticapps@gmail.com  
**Subject:** Delete my 75 Hard Social account

Include:
- Your account email address
- (Optional) Your in-app display name

We may ask you to verify ownership of the account before deletion.

## What data is deleted
When your deletion is processed, we delete:
- Your authentication account (login)
- Your profile information (name, email, profile photo)
- Your app data stored in our database (challenge/progress data)
- Your uploaded photos stored for the app

## What data may be retained
We may retain limited information if required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.
If retained, it will be kept only as long as necessary.

## Processing time
We aim to complete deletion requests within **14 days**.
